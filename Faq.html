Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most asked questions:

1.What is Nyumbani? How does it work?

In one sentence, Nyumbani Student Residences is Kenya's largest network of purpose-built student accommodation in Nairobi. In detail, Nyumbani is a first-of-its-kind, technology-driven and professionally-managed brand of student accommodation, that is changing the way students live in Nairobi City. Nyumbani Student Residences is designed for young students who are looking for a place that is designed specifically for them, keeping their needs, comforts, and aspirations in mind. A place that can become your second home.

2. Who can live at Nyumbani?

Any student studying at a University or College in Nairobi, who can certify that: he/she is currently enrolled as a student and is taking one or more courses. he/she is registered to take classes the following semester. he/she has not been convicted of any offenses that are punishable by the laws of Kenya.

3. Can I live with a friend?

Of course! If availability at the time of reservation allows, you can both book the same room type, in the same building. When making your reservation, you can alert our Customer Service team on the same so we can match the bookings together and process as a joint application.

4. Is there a curfew?

No, there is no curfew. Our residences have 24-hour front entrance security and residents can enter with their Nyumbani Access Card whenever they please. However, most residences within Nairobi City have quiet hours from 10pm-8am. During final exams, we may have 24-hour quiet hours in effect.

5. Can I schedule a tour of the property before I book?

Yes, of course. You can contact us to arrange a viewing of our property.
